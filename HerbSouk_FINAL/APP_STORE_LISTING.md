☯ THE FIRST MEDICINE PHILOSOPHY

In Western medicine, illness is treated by suppressing symptoms. A skin condition gets steroids. Anxiety gets benzodiazepines. Pain gets anti-inflammatories. The body's natural intelligence is overridden.

TCM — First Medicine — treats the root cause (the ben), not just the symptoms (the biao). Every herb is understood through its energetic properties: its temperature, its flavour, and its affinity for specific organ systems. This is not metaphor — it is a sophisticated clinical system refined over millennia.

The Herb Souk gives you both perspectives. Understand WHY ginger warms the Middle Jiao and stops nausea — and understand how its gingerols block the 5-HT3 receptor in exactly the same way pharmaceutical anti-emetics do. Both are true. Both are useful.

────────────────────────

💰 COMMERCIAL APPLICATIONS

Each herb profile includes a "Commercial Value" section showing what products the herb is used in — from FDA-approved topical capsaicin patches, to Sambucol elderberry syrup, to Silexan lavender capsules that outperformed lorazepam in clinical trials. Understanding the commercial landscape of each herb makes this app useful for health professionals, natural product entrepreneurs, and informed consumers alike.
