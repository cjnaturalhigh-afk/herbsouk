☯ A note on chronic conditions and treatment duration
Traditional Chinese Medicine treats the root cause — the ben — of chronic conditions such as PCOS, recurrent miscarriage, endometriosis, and fertility challenges. Because we are addressing underlying patterns rather than suppressing symptoms, meaningful clinical change takes time.

All programmes begin with a minimum of 12 sessions as a clinical foundation. The number of sessions required beyond this varies significantly between individuals and cannot be predetermined at the outset. Progress is reviewed continuously and your practitioner will advise you honestly at each stage. There are no fixed resolution timeframes for chronic conditions — only a commitment to genuine, root-cause treatment.
