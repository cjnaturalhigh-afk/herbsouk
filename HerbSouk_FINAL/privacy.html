The Herb Souk uses localStorage (not cookies) to store your language preference and unlock status on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us. Google Analytics uses cookies for anonymous usage tracking — you may block these via your browser settings.

We use HTTPS encryption for all data transmission. Payment data is handled exclusively by Stripe and is never stored on our servers. Unlock code validation is handled server-side via Netlify Functions.
