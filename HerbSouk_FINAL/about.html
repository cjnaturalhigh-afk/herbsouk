What is First Medicine™?

Traditional Chinese Medicine predates Western medicine by approximately 6,000 years. It is not alternative medicine, complementary medicine, or folk medicine. It is the original medicine — refined across thousands of years of clinical observation.

"Western medicine treats the biao — the symptom. First Medicine treats the ben — the root cause. The body is not broken. It is communicating. Learn to listen."

Every herb in The Herb Souk is understood through its energetic properties: its temperature (hot, warm, neutral, cool, cold), its flavour (pungent, sweet, sour, bitter, salty), and its affinity for specific organ systems. This is the clinical framework used for six millennia.

The Herb Souk™ — Your Supermarket Pharmacy

The herbs and spices in your local supermarket are the same herbs used in Chinese medicine for thousands of years. Ginger for 49p. Turmeric for 89p. Cinnamon for 99p.

The Herb Souk reveals their profound healing power — through two lenses simultaneously: the ancient TCM clinical action, and the modern Western scientific evidence that is now catching up with what First Medicine knew millennia ago.

Every herb profile includes the Chinese name, pinyin pronunciation, TCM properties, Western clinical evidence, commercial applications, and cautions. Available in 12 languages through AI-powered real-time translation.
